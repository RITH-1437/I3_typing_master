Our Story

TypingMaster was founded in 2020 by a team of educators and developers who recognized the growing importance of digital literacy. We noticed that many people, despite spending hours daily on computers, never learned to type efficiently.

Starting as a simple typing test website, we've evolved into a comprehensive platform that serves thousands of users worldwide. Our commitment to continuous improvement drives us to regularly update our features based on user feedback and the latest research in learning methodologies.
